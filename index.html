About Us

Dhar was founded in 2020 by a passionate group of animal advocates in San Francisco, California. Our mission is to fight against the cruelty of puppy mills and promote ethical breeding and adoption practices.

Our Mission

Our mission is to eradicate puppy mills by raising awareness, supporting ethical adoption, and advocating for stronger animal welfare laws. We believe every dog deserves a loving and caring home.

How We Help

At Dhar, we take a multi-faceted approach to fight puppy mills:

  • Organizing public awareness campaigns and educational workshops.
  • Partnering with ethical breeders and adoption centers.
  • Providing resources and support for puppy mill survivors.
  • Lobbying for stronger animal protection laws.
