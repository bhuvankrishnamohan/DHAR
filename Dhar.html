About Us

Dhar is a dedicated organization committed to advocating against puppy mills. We work tirelessly to raise awareness, promote humane breeding practices, and support legislative changes to end the cruelty of mass breeding facilities.

Our Mission

Our mission is to eradicate puppy mills by educating the public about their impact, supporting ethical pet adoption, and pushing for stronger animal welfare laws. We believe in a world where all dogs are treated with the love, care, and respect they deserve.

How We Help

Dhar helps by:

  • Raising public awareness through campaigns and workshops.
  • Providing resources for adopting pets from ethical sources.
  • Supporting victims of puppy mills and helping them find forever homes.
  • Advocating for stricter animal welfare laws.
